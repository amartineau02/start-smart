Q1

Is this app only available for Android?

Yes, but it will be available for iOS by summer of 2020!

Q2

For notes, motivations, and habits, is there a character limit?

Not at all! We feel it is necessary to allow you to have the freedom to write as much or as little as you need.

Q3

If I uninstall the app, will I lose my progress?

It is a possibility, but if you sign up with your email, all information will (securely and privately) be saved to the Start Smart Cloud, that way you don’t lose progress, notes, or motivations.
